About Me.

Hi! I'm a retoucher and photo editor based in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. I believe that commercial success starts from professional and eye-catching images, that needs to be treated with attention from the start (photography) to the end (post-processing). Whether you're a start-up looking to turn your grand brand ideas into a reality or a well established brand looking for a refresh, my goal is to help you. I will learn to understand the personality and attributes behind your brand/product, so I can get the essence of the brand across in the editing and retouching. That's why I'm specialized in photography that give a high response (marketplace, lifestyle, wedding, product) and photo editing that gives the indispensable high-end look treatment. Something I love to do is paying attention to clients necessity, that I always put first. I'd love to hear your project, find a solution together and deliver the best result. I’ll fully project manage your brief from start to finish also I'm always try complete orders on deadline.

Vitkac retouch

www.vitkac.com

Retouching for the Vitkac website is a professional photo editing process to meet the high standards of luxury retail. The main tasks of a retoucher include: 1. Color correction – ensuring accurate color representation so that the image matches the real product. 2. Removing defects – fixing wrinkles, dust, glare, and other imperfections on clothing, shoes, and accessories. 3. Background cleanup – creating a clean, uniform background (usually white or gray) for a consistent product display. 4. Texture enhancement – preserving the natural look of fabrics, leather, and other materials. 5. Cropping and alignment – adjusting the image to the required format so that the products appear neat and proportionate. 6. Web optimization – reducing file size without quality loss to ensure fast website loading. Project Link.
